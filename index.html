Deconstruction

Maison Margiela is known for its deconstructed silhouettes, exposed seams, and unconventional tailoring. The brand challenges traditional fashion rules.

Iconic Tabi Boots

The Tabi boot remains one of the house's most recognizable designs. Inspired by Japanese split-toe socks, it represents Margiela’s experimental aesthetic.

Anonymous Identity

The brand is famous for anonymity. Models often wear masks during shows, shifting focus from the individual to the garments themselves.
